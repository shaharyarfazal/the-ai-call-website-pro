## What is Make.com?

Make.com (formerly Integromat) is a **visual workflow automation platform** that connects your favorite apps and services without writing a single line of code.

Think of it as the ultimate bridge between your tools:
- **CRM** → Slack notifications
- **Google Sheets** → Email campaigns
- **Shopify orders** → Accounting software
- **Form submissions** → WhatsApp messages
- **Calendar events** → Team reminders

### The Magic: Visual "Scenarios"

Unlike traditional automation tools that use linear "if-this-then-that" logic, Make.com uses **visual scenarios** — drag-and-drop workflows where you can see exactly how data flows between apps.

```

    ↓
[Add to Google Sheets]
    ↓
[Send Welcome Email via Gmail]
    ↓
[Notify Sales Team on Slack]
    ↓
[Create Task in Trello]
```

All of this happens **automatically, 24/7**, while you focus on growing your business.
